Get Involved

You can make a difference in the lives of underprivileged students. Here's how you can get Involved, we believe that creating change requires a collective effort. You can make a difference by joining us in our mission to empower underprivileged students by selecting one of the below option.

Become a Mentor

Join our team of dedicated mentors, utilize your own professional education and experience and help shape the future of a student. Share expertise, provide guidance, and be a source of inspiration for them. Time commitment is 30-60 mins, once a week or bi-weekly depending on student's needs.

Join Us

Outreach Coordinators

As an outreach coordinator, you will establish and maintain relationships with schools. Your role will involve reaching out to schools, discussing our mentoring and coaching programs, and identifying students who would benefit from our services

Join Us

Volunteer

We welcome volunteers to assist with workshops, events, and administrative tasks. Your time and support can have a significant impact on our students' journeys. College students are welcome to apply. Time commitment is 60 mins once a week.

Join Us

Donate

Your generous contributions allow us to expand our programs, reach more students, and provide them with the resources they need to succeed. Every donation, no matter the size, makes a difference.

Join Us

Spread the Word

Help us reach more students and potential supporters by sharing our mission on social media, within your community, and through word of mouth.
Join us at speak2mentor and be part of a community dedicated to empowering underprivileged students. Together, we can make a lasting impact, inspiring and guiding these young individuals to achieve their full potential and create a brighter future for themselves and their communities
Even a single conversation or the right words of wisdom shared can the trajectory of someone's life's course. Get started today and let's shape futures together!
