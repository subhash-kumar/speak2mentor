Privacy Policy for speak2mentor

At speak2mentor, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our website visitors, program participants, and donors. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. By using our website and participating in our program, you consent to the terms of this policy.

Information We Collect

speak2mentor collects personal information from our website visitors, program participants, and donors. This information includes:

  • Name, email address, phone number, and mailing address
  • Date of birth
  • School, grade level, and academic interests
  • Parent or guardian contact information
  • Donor information, including name, address, and payment details

How We Use Your Information

speak2mentor uses your personal information to provide and improve our mentoring program, communicate with you, and process donations. Specifically, we may use your information for the following purposes:

  • To match students with mentors based on shared interests and availability
  • To communicate with program participants and their parents or guardians about program updates, events, and scheduling
  • To process donations and provide donor acknowledgments
  • To improve our program by analyzing participant demographics and feedback

How We Protect Your Information

speak2mentor takes reasonable precautions to protect your personal information from loss, theft, unauthorized access, and misuse. We use industry-standard encryption and secure storage to protect your information. However, please note that no data transmission over the internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

Sharing Your Information

speak2mentor will not sell, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with our trusted service providers, such as website hosting companies, payment processors, and email communication platforms, for the purpose of providing our services to you.

Legal Disclaimer

speak2mentor may disclose your personal information if required to do so by law, or if we believe in good faith that such action is necessary to comply with a legal obligation, protect and defend our rights or property, or prevent fraud or other illegal activity.

Updates to This Policy

speak2mentor may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on our website, and your continued use of our website and participation in our program following the posting of changes constitutes your acceptance of such changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at admin@speak2mentor.org
